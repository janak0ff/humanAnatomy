Respiratory system

Respiration, the process of exchange of gases between the environment and the human body involves three types of respiratory mechanisms namely, external, internal and cellular respiration, all of which have been elucidated using graphic 3D animations in this Atlas.

The Essential Animated Atlas of the Respiratory system includes the following topics:

  • Respiration
  • Anatomy of respiratory passages
  • Conducting part
  • Lungs
  • Mechanism of Respiration
  • Mechanism of inspiration
  • Mechanism of expiration
  • Cellular Respiration
  • Mechanism of gas exchange
